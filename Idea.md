Hackathon Theme - "Smart Campus"

Interviewed with Marshall, IT on campus. An issue he had was that he had access to an AI facial recognition system to put names to faces on the security camera systems, but they are not able to use it because it doesn't meet the standards - namely, it doesn't encrypt the video camera data at rest. So the facial detection is unusable, which prevents detection of security threats.

Another idea we had is making a heat map of noise levels of rooms on campus, so that students can know what places are quiet study spaces vs. recreational or loud areas.

We propose our project, CougPulse, which solves the issue of security threat detection for IT and room finding for students. We will use a phone as both a camera and microphone to collect audio levels and detect faces. The data (video and audio) we collect MUST be encrypted at rest to solve the IT problem of meeting standards for use. There is a web app for presenting the heat map of the rooms. It could also predict the audio levels of rooms based on past data, for example, the Planet Fitness 'Crowd History' ![alt text](Screenshot_20260403-144421.png)
